About Us

We are FTC Team 48: NerdHerd Locked In, coming from the California Academy of Mathematics and Science, a public magnet high school on the campus of California State University, Dominguez Hills. Our students have designed, created, programmed, competed, and collaborated since our team was established in 2007.

We compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), which is a robotics competition in which middle and high school teams across the world compete in an annual challenge. These games are designed to test our engineering abilities by tasking us with creating robots that accomplish challenging tasks. Teams come together at events to team up in alliances and compete against one another.
